The used measurements are

Length

  • 1 inch = 2.54 centimeters
  • 1 foot = 30.48 centimeters
  • 1 yard = 0.9144 meters
  • 1 mile = 1.609 kilometers
  • 1 centimeter = 0.3937 inches
  • 1 meter = 3.281 feet
  • 1 kilometer = 0.621 miles

Weight

  • 1 ounce = 28.3495 grams
  • 1 pound = 0.4536 kilograms
  • 1 gram = 0.0353 ounces
  • 1 kilogram = 2.205 pounds

Temperature

  • Celsius to Fahrenheit: °F = °C × (9/5) + 32
  • Fahrenheit to Celsius: °C = (°F − 32) × (5/9)

Speed

  • 1 mile per hour (mph) = 1.609 kilometers per hour (kph)
  • 1 kilometer per hour (kph) = 0.621 miles per hour (mph)
  • 1 meter per second (m/s) = 3.6 kilometers per hour (kph)
  • 1 foot per second (ft/s) = 1.097 kilometers per hour (kph)

Volume

  • 1 teaspoon = 4.9289 milliliters
  • 1 tablespoon = 14.7868 milliliters
  • 1 fluid ounce = 29.5735 milliliters
  • 1 cup = 236.588 milliliters
  • 1 pint = 0.4732 liters
  • 1 quart = 0.9464 liters
  • 1 gallon = 3.7854 liters
  • 1 milliliter = 0.2029 teaspoons
  • 1 liter = 2.1134 pints

Area

  • 1 square inch = 6.4516 square centimeters
  • 1 square foot = 0.0929 square meters
  • 1 square yard = 0.8361 square meters
  • 1 square mile = 2.59 square kilometers
  • 1 acre = 0.4047 hectares
  • 1 square centimeter = 0.1550 square inches
  • 1 square meter = 10.764 square feet
  • 1 square kilometer = 0.3861 square miles
  • 1 hectare = 2.4711 acres

Time

  • 1 minute = 60 seconds
  • 1 hour = 60 minutes
  • 1 day = 24 hours
  • 1 week = 7 days
  • 1 year = 365 days (or 366 days in a leap year)
